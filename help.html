How to Get an Area Risk Assessment

Most Popular Cities: New York City, NY Los Angeles, CA Chicago, IL Houston, TX Phoenix, AZ Philadelphia, PA San Antonio, TX San Diego, CA Dallas, TX San Jose, CA
    • Start by entering the city to be searched for in the box labeled "City:"
    • Next enter the two letter state abbreviation in the box labeled "State:"
    • Then click the submit button to generate the assessment of the entered location
    • Alternatively choose an entry from the list of most popular cities to get an assessment for the selected choice

    • Once the assessment has been generated, you will be redirected to the results page
    • One the results page you will find:
      • A dropdown with Crime Data about the entered city if available
      • A dropdown detailing Natural Disaster data in the state searched for
      • ...OTHER DATA...
      • To open a dropdown click on the category (ex. Crime Data ▼) and the information will be displayed
      • To close a dropdown again click on the category (ex. Natural Disasters ▼) and the information will be hidden

    • New searches can be done by again entering city and state abbreviation in the top right of the page and pressing submit
    • Return to the home page by clicking the home icon in the top left of the page
